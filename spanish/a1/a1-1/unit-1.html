Greetings, alphabet and first sounds

1 Hook / Opener课程导入

📷 Suggested image: two people meeting for the first time on a video call — one in Spain, one in China. Warm, modern, friendly.

Before we start — think about this:

开始之前，思考一下：

  • How do you greet someone in the morning in Chinese? And in the evening?
  • In Spanish, the greeting changes depending on the time of day. Do you know any Spanish greetings already?
  • How do you say "nice to meet you" in English? Spanish has three different ways.
  • 在中文里，早上和晚上分别怎么打招呼？
  • 西班牙语中，问候语会因时间不同而变化。你已经知道哪些西班牙语问候语了吗？
  • 英语中怎么说"很高兴认识你"？西班牙语有三种不同的说法。

By the end of this unit you can:完成本单元后，你能够： greet anyone in Spanish at any time of day, introduce yourself, spell your name aloud, and survive your first online Spanish lesson.用西班牙语在任意时间与任何人打招呼、自我介绍、大声拼出自己的名字，以及顺利完成第一堂在线西班牙语课。

3 Grammar in context语法讲解

Part 1 — Tú vs. Usted (你 vs. 您)第一部分 — Tú 与 Usted (你 vs. 您)

Spanish has two ways to say "you" — and choosing the wrong one can feel rude.

西班牙语中有两种表达"你"的方式——选错了会让人感到不礼貌。

TúUsted
Use with使用场合 Friends, family, classmates, people your age朋友、家人、同学、同龄人 Boss, teacher, doctor, older people, strangers上司、老师、医生、年长者、陌生人
Feeling语气感觉 Casual, warm, friendly随意、亲切、友好 Respectful, formal尊重、正式
Quick test判断技巧 Would you use their first name?你会直接叫对方的名字吗？ Would you say señor/señora?你会称呼对方为 señor/señora 吗？

Real examples:

实际例句：

  • ¿Tú eres Ana? — (asking a classmate)（问同学时使用）
  • ¿Usted es la profesora Lin? — (addressing a teacher)（称呼老师时使用）
  • Wei le dice a Carlos: "¿Cómo estás tú?" — (they are friends)（他们是朋友）

⚠️ Note (注意): In Latin America, usted is used even more than in Spain — sometimes even with people your own age in formal situations.

⚠️ 注意：在拉丁美洲，usted 的使用比西班牙更普遍——有时甚至在与同龄人交流的正式场合也会使用。

💡 Good news: In Spanish you can often drop the pronoun — the verb ending already tells you who is speaking. Soy Ana is more natural than Yo soy Ana. As a beginner, keeping the pronoun is completely fine.

💡 好消息：西班牙语中通常可以省略人称代词——动词变位已经说明了主语是谁。Soy Ana 比 Yo soy Ana 更自然。初学者保留人称代词完全没问题。

4 Reading or dialogue阅读与对话

📷 Suggested image: two people on a video call, one in a modern apartment, one in a café — smiling, relaxed.

Primera conversación

Wei and Ana meet in an online Spanish course for the first time. Read carefully.

Wei 和 Ana 第一次在线上西班牙语课相识。请仔细阅读。

Ana: Hola, buenos días. ¿Cómo te llamas?

Wei: Hola. Me llamo Wei. ¿Y tú?

Ana: Me llamo Ana. Mucho gusto, Wei.

Wei: Igualmente. ¿De dónde eres, Ana?

Ana: Soy de España, de Madrid. ¿Y tú?

Wei: Yo soy de China, de Shanghái. ¡Encantado!

Ana: ¡Encantada! Oye, ¿cómo se escribe tu nombre?

Wei: W - E - I. ¿Y el tuyo?

Ana: A - N - A. ¡Muy fácil!

Wei: (ríe) Sí. Bueno, ¡hasta luego, Ana!

Ana: ¡Hasta luego! ¡Hasta la próxima clase!

Comprehension questions理解问题

Answer in Spanish:

用西班牙语回答：

  1. ¿Cómo se llama el estudiante de China?
  2. ¿De dónde es Ana exactamente?
  3. ¿Por qué Wei dice ¡Encantado! y Ana dice ¡Encantada!?
  4. ¿Cómo se despiden al final?
  5. ¿Qué frase usa Ana para decir "nice to meet you"?

6 Question formation如何提问

Most students learn to answer questions. In this class, you also learn to ask them. A conversation only works if both people can ask questions.

大多数学生只学会回答问题。在这节课上，你也要学会提问。对话的前提是双方都能提问。

7 Real-life task真实任务

📷 Suggested image: a phone screen showing a WeChat contact — someone about to start an online call.

Task: Your first Spanish introduction任务：你的第一次西班牙语自我介绍

Imagine you are joining a new online Spanish group class with students from different countries. You need to introduce yourself to the group.

想象你正在加入一个新的在线西班牙语群课，同学来自不同国家。你需要向大家介绍自己。

Step 1 — Prepare your introduction:第一步 — 准备你的自我介绍： Answer these questions in Spanish:用西班牙语回答以下问题：

  • ¿Cómo te llamas?
  • ¿De dónde eres?
  • ¿Cómo estás hoy?

Step 2 — Spell your name:第二步 — 拼读你的名字： Using the Spanish alphabet, spell your name out loud. Your teacher will write it as they hear it — did they get it right?用西班牙语字母大声拼出你的名字。老师会边听边写——他们写对了吗？

Step 3 — Greet and leave:第三步 — 问候与告别： Practice greeting your teacher for three different times of day:练习在以下三个不同时间段向老师打招呼：

  • 8:00 a.m.
  • 4:00 p.m.
  • 9:30 p.m.

Step 4 — Real life:第四步 — 真实生活： Open WhatsApp or WeChat and type a greeting message to someone in Spanish right now. Show your teacher what you wrote.现在就打开 WhatsApp 或微信，用西班牙语发一条问候消息给某人。把你写的内容展示给老师看。

8 Free production自由表达

No script. No word bank. Just you, speaking freely using everything from this unit.

没有脚本，没有词汇表。用你在本单元学到的一切自由表达。

Speaking prompts口语提示

  1. Introduce yourself fully in Spanish — name, where you are from, how you are today.
  2. How do you greet different people in your life? Your boss? Your friend? Your parent? Would you use tú or usted with each one?
  3. You meet someone Spanish-speaking for the first time at work. What do you say? Act it out from start to finish — greeting, introduction, farewell.
  4. What was the hardest thing in this unit? Can you explain it in English, and then try to say the correct version in Spanish?
  1. 用西班牙语完整介绍自己——姓名、来自哪里、今天状态如何。
  2. 你如何向生活中的不同人打招呼？你的上司？朋友？父母？对每个人你会用 tú 还是 usted？
  3. 你在工作中第一次遇到一位说西班牙语的人。你会说什么？从头到尾演练一遍——问候、介绍、告别。
  4. 本单元最难的是什么？你能先用英语或中文解释一下，然后尝试用西班牙语说出正确版本吗？

💡 Remember: mistakes are part of learning. The goal here is not to be perfect — it is to communicate.

💡 记住：犯错是学习的一部分。这里的目标不是完美——而是能够沟通。
